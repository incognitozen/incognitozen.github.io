Fyrebox provides tools to create a quiz to generate leads, to educate or simply to engage your audience.

Make your quiz to generate leads. An interactive quiz is a great way to generate leads on your website, landing page or social media.

You can use the last page of your quiz to encourage an action. For instance, you can redirect the participants to your Facebook page, or a page of your website.
