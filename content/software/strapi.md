<p>Strapi is a free and open-source headless CMS delivering your content anywhere you need.</p>
<ul>
<li>Keep control over your data. With Strapi, you know where your data is stored, and you keep full control at all times.</li>
<li>Self-hosted. You can host and scale Strapi projects the way you want. You can choose any hosting platform you want: AWS, Render, Heroku, a VPS, or a dedicated server. Y<span style="font-size: revert; color: initial;">ou can scale as you grow, 100% independent.</span></li>
<li>Database agnostic. You can choose the database you prefer. Strapi works with SQL &amp; NoSQL databases: MongoDB, <a href="https://hackerspad.net/software/postgresql/">PostgreSQL</a>, <a href="https://hackerspad.net/software/mysql/">MySQL</a>, <a href="https://hackerspad.net/software/mariadb/">MariaDB</a>, and <a href="https://hackerspad.net/software/sqlite/">SQLite</a>.</li>
<li>Customizable. You can quickly build your logic by fully customizing APIs, routes, or plugins to fit your needs perfectly.</li>
</ul>
