Decentralized password manager for teams on Blockstack. Blockvault is a decentralized alternative to <a href="https://hackerspad.net/software/1password/">1Password</a>. Keep your passwords and credit cards secured with the power of Blockstack. Blockvault runs exclusively on your device (client-side). There are no central servers that can be hacked or breached. <span style="font-size: inherit;">You control where your data is hosted. Blockvault does not store or replicate user data. Your data is end-to-end encrypted with keys that only you hold.</span>
