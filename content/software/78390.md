Topgrading Online Solutions (TOLS) enables companies to automate several critical aspects of their hiring. Within TOLS, job candidates provide information about their two most recent jobs, and TOLS produces an overview of the candidates which you can quickly scan to select the best candidates to move forward without having to read through countless resumes.
