Comprehensive suite of Safety Management tools to make the life for the Safety Manager easier.  Includes Safety Observation reporting, Accident and Near-Miss Reporting, Electronic Forms/Inspections, Document Management, SDS Management, Training and Certification Tracking, Video Library Management, Automatic Safety Participation Tracking, Reporting Dashboards, OSHA reporting, and a mobile app with online and offline capabilities.
