Proqura is an intuitive and affordable procure-to-pay software for small and medium sized businesses. Managing procurement processes has never been easier: From streamlining internal workflows to managing suppliers, Proqura provides all the procurement tools a company needs to requisition, communicate, negotiate and transact with suppliers in real time, all in one place.
<ul class="bulleted">
 	<li>Create and approve purchase requests</li>
 	<li>Create and float RFQs and RFPs to suppliers</li>
 	<li>Receive bids from suppliers electronically</li>
 	<li>Easily compare and evaluate offers</li>
 	<li>Negotiate with suppliers</li>
 	<li>Approve and issue purchase orders</li>
 	<li>Track and receive delivery</li>
 	<li>Match invoices and pay bills</li>
 	<li>Easily onboard and manage suppliers</li>
 	<li>Curate and maintain catalogs of frequently purchases items</li>
</ul>
