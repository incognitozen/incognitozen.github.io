Exolix is a secure and anonymous crypto exchange service with fixed rates which is free of registration and any limits. Launched in 2018, Exolix gained recognition as a fast and secure exchange. Exolix provides its customers with the highest standards of confidentiality, as you do not have to register to make a transaction. The system also fixes the exchange rate at the beginning of the transaction, thus, users are protected from market volatility. In addition, Exolix does not have maximum limits, which allows everyone to exchange any amount of cryptocurrencies.
The main mission of Exolix is to make an exchange process fast, easy and secure and Exolix fulfils this intent with dignity.
