It is a standalone multi-vendor software that has been used by 1000+ businesses including few billion-dollar businesses from various industries including precious metals, FMCG, etc.

Apart from a robust marketplace packed with eCommerce essentials, the platform offers complete ownership with a lifetime license, flexible payment terms, and free technical support for 12 months.
