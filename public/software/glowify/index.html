Glowify

Glowify is a mobile application service enabling users to find and book verified beauty professionals in their area for a variety of occasions. Users can chat to hair stylists & makeup artists, view portfolios and reviews and manage their calendar, all from within the app to ensure a seamless beauty booking experience every time.

  • Find and book top beauty professionals near you.
  • View individual artist portfolios.
  • In-built messaging.
  • Trial booking before commitment.
  • Artist calendar management.
