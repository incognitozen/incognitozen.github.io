Amazon Appstore

The Amazon Appstore is a place to purchase and download apps for your Android device.

AppCake

AppCake is a website and mobile app that gives you a place to download apps for your iPhone from. The site features all sorts of apps, from games to productivity based apps.

Aptoide

Aptoide is a third party replacement for the traditional Google Play Store.

F-Droid

F-Droid is an interesting alternative for Android users who want to try something different and not have to use the Goole Play store all the time.

Fossdroid

The project’s aim is to promote free and open source software on the Android platform.

GetApk

GetApk is among those leading app stores that in addition to providing the apps for the Android mobile devices provide the apps for the Android tablets and other Android based devices as well.

GetJar

Discover and play paid apps and games for free.

LG SmartWorld

LG SmartWorld is an LG Android device centric alternative to the Google Play Store. It is used often with LG TVs as well as their own phones and tablets.

Mobogenie

Mobogenie serves as a method for connecting your Android device to a computer and filing aspects of your phone like files, contacts, and apps.

Uptodown

Uptodown is a mobile app and website that lets you download games and apps to users on most of the major platforms.

Yalp Store

Yalp Store lets you download apps from Google Play Store as apk files.
