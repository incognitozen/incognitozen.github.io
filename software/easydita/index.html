easyDITA is a real-time collaboration solution for authoring, managing, and publishing content to multiple channels in multiple languages for any audience.

easyDITA reduces the cost and complexity of delivering the right content to your customers in the right format and language. easyDITA's Component Content Management System (CCMS) is familiar and comfortable, making it easy for non-technical people to write structured content. Centrally locate your content in a single repository where they can be securely accessed for discussion, review, and approval. When you're ready to publish, easyDITA helps you get the word out on all available channels – print, web, online help, and mobile.
