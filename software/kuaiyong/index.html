Kuaiyong

Kuaiyong brings features to install free as well as paid apps on Apple devices without jailbreaking them.

8 Alternatives To Kuaiyong

25pp

25pp is App Store that contains exclusive apps that are not yet available on app stores.

AppAddict

AppAddict is an online tool that allows you to download third-party apps on your devices without rooting or jailbreaking your devices.

AppTrackr

Find more and search less with the TrackR app.

Emus4u

Alternative third-party app store for iOS devices.

HiPStore

HiPStore is an app store for Android and iOS cell phones that allows you to download premium apps without paying any price.

iNoJB

iNoJB, formerly iNoCydia, is an amazing Cydia alternative requiring no jailbreak!

iNuclearApp

iNuclearApp is an iOS emulator which runs tweaks, etc.
