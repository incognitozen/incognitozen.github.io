Gamerate

Gamerate is social based database of computer, electronic and board games.

Goodfilms

A social film review site, focusing on what your friends are watching, and what they thought of it.

IMDb

Internet Movie Database

JustWatch

Compare prices of streaming for movies and tv shows.

Letterboxd

Letterboxd is a social site for sharing your taste in film, now in public beta.

Listal

Listal is a web-based DVD, music, game, book and many other things collection manager.

OpenCritic

OpenCritic is basically a community-based website that aggregates the news, reviews and views on a game on a single platform.

Rotten Tomatoes

Rotten Tomatoes, home of the Tomatometer, is the most trusted measurement of quality for Movies & TV. The definitive site for Reviews, Trailers, Showtimes, and Tickets

TasteDive

TasteDive recommends similar music (musicians, bands), movies, TV shows, books, authors and games, based on what you like.

alaTest

alaTest provides Reviews, Ratings and Price Comparison for a wide range of products: TV, Cell Phones, Digital Cameras, Computers, GPS, and more.
