Blockvault

Decentralized password manager for teams on Blockstack. Blockvault is a decentralized alternative to 1Password. Keep your passwords and credit cards secured with the power of Blockstack. Blockvault runs exclusively on your device (client-side). There are no central servers that can be hacked or breached. You control where your data is hosted. Blockvault does not store or replicate user data. Your data is end-to-end encrypted with keys that only you hold.
