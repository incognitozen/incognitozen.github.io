SliceCrafter

SliceCrafter is an online version of slicing technology that is less powerful than IceSL-slicer but runs completely from within the web browser.

16 Alternatives To SliceCrafter

3D Slash

3D Slash is another 3D modeling solution that offers an original and intuitive experience to create and design 3D model easily.

Autodesk Fusion 360

Integrated CAD, CAM, and CAE featuring collaborative editing and cloud-based computation.

Cupix

Cupix is an open-source state of the art 3d solution providing platform for any space that gives you a complete 3d design.

KISSlicer

KISSlicer is a powerful, easy-to-use, ​and fast cross-platform app that slices STL files into printer-ready G-code files. ​ ​ Keep It Simple Slicer - KISS!

MakePrintable

MakePrintable.com created by Mixed Dimensions is a cloud solution that analyses and repairs any 3D model for 3Dprinting.

MatterControl

MatterControl is a free, open source app that lets you organize and manage your 3D prints.

OctoPrint

OctoPrint is the snappy web interface for your 3D printer that allows you to control and monitor all aspects of your printer and print jobs, right from your browser.

Onshape

Onshape is the first full-cloud 3D CAD system. It runs in a web browser and on any mobile device.

SketchUp Free

SketchUp Free is an impressive 3D modeling software solution that comes as a reliable application to create a 3D model of furniture, interiors, landscapes and much more.
