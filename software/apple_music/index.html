Apple Music

Apple Music app combines your personal iTunes library with Apple’s music subscription service. Music you have purchased from the iTunes store, or synced over from other sources, is available in the “Library” tab. Read more about Apple Music.

18 Alternatives To Apple Music

Deezer

Deezer is a music streaming app created in France. It is available in 180 counties and gets 16 million users a month. 6 million of the users have paid subscriptions. Read more about Deezer.

Grooveshark

Grooveshark was a popular service for streaming music that was created in 2006. The platform allowed individuals to stream audio files that were uploaded by other users. Read more about Grooveshark.

Jamendo

Jamendo app is a free Android music player that allows you to download and listen to music legally for free on your mobile device.
