17 Alternatives To Apple iTunes

Amazon Prime Music

Amazon Music is one or probably the most popular music streaming application which enables its global users to enjoy their most likely music right through their cell phones and tablets.

Apple Music

Apple Music app combines your personal iTunes library with Apple’s music subscription service. Music you have purchased from the iTunes store, or synced over from other sources, is available in the “Library” tab.

Deezer

Deezer is a music streaming app created in France. It is available in 180 counties and gets 16 million users a month. 6 million of the users have paid subscriptions. Read more about Deezer.

Ecoute

Ecoute is a standalone music player tool that is specially made for Mac OS and iOS users, created by Louka Desroziers and Julien Sagot.

Grooveshark

Grooveshark was a popular service for streaming music that was created in 2006. The platform allowed individuals to stream audio files that were uploaded by other users. Read more about Grooveshark.

MusicBee

While iTunes might be the benchmark music organizer and player app, MusicBee is a strong alternative that many prefer.

PodTrans

PodTrans is a user-friendly software that let you to transfer music to your iPod and create a backup of your audio files on your personal computer.
