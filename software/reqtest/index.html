ReQtest

ReQtest is an end to end solution for test management, bug tracking, requirement management and agile project management. ReQtest consists of a suite of modules: Requirements, Tests, Agile Board, Bugs, & Reports. ReQtest also offers Capture; a downloadable tool for screen capture.

  • Organize, review & prioritize requirements intuitively
  • Well suited for Agile requirements management
  • Review & track all changes instantly
  • Highly customizable requirement module
  • Integrated test suite to plan, execute and follow up test cases
  • Tailor-made to support agile test case management
  • Intuitive test management process
  • Aggregated reporting to gain actionable insights
  • Effortlessly capture, track & manage bugs and issues
  • Preview bug reports without any clicks
  • Charts for visual presentation of bug reports
  • Built-in filters to quickly find specific bug reports
  • Gain a holistic view of your project’s progress
  • Link to requirements, bug reports, and test tasks
  • Find bottlenecks in the project quickly
  • Collaborate, communicate & coordinate with agile board
